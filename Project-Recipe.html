Recipe for Efo Riro

Efo-riro is popular and known as the Vegetable soup amongst the Yoruba people of Nigeria. It is originally made with either “Efo tete” (Spinach) or Soko leaves. Although waterleaf (or “Gure” in youruba language) can also be added.

Ingredients Quantity
Vegetables 3 bundles
Oil 1 bowl
Beef 2kg
Maggi 2cubes

Steps To Prepare Efo Riro

  1. Cut the beef rinse and throw into a pot.
  2. Season with Salt, bullion powder (or stock cubes)
  3. Add the diced onions and paper and allow to boil
  4. Wash and slice the vegetables
  5. Add other ingredients once the meat and fish become tender
  6. Add the Washed vegetables
  7. In 2 minutes the soup is ready to be served

Reciepe for Ogbono

Ogbono is a rich, nutrition-packed Nigerian soup made from ogbono seeds (ground African mango seeds), palm oil, assorted meats, and traditional spices.
It is called 'Draw Soup', due to the mucilaginous nature that gives it a slippery and viscous texture

Ingredients Quantity
Ogbono 1 cup
Oil 1 bowl
Beef 2kg
Maggi 2cubes

Steps To Prepare Ogbono

  1. Cut the beef rinse and throw into a pot.
  2. Season with Salt, bullion powder (or stock cubes)
  3. Add the diced onions and paper and allow to boil
  4. Add the blended Ogbono once your meat and fish become tender
  5. Stir to dissolve the Ogbono
  6. Add all the ingredients and allow to boil
  7. add vegetable on low heat
